## Things to Do in Nosara

Nosara, located on the stunning Pacific coast of Costa Rica, offers an array of activities for every type of traveler. Whether you're a nature enthusiast, a beach lover, or someone seeking adventure, Nosara has something to offer. Here are some of the top things to do when you visit:

###### 1. Surfing at Playa Guiones

Playa Guiones is a world-renowned surf destination, known for its consistent waves that cater to all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced surfer, this beach is perfect for catching some waves and enjoying the laid-back vibe of Nosara.

###### 2. Yoga and Wellness Retreats

Nosara is a hub for yoga and wellness retreats. With its serene beaches and lush jungle surroundings, it's the ideal place to unwind and rejuvenate. Many retreats offer week-long programs that focus on yoga, meditation, and holistic healing practices.

###### 3. Explore the Nosara Wildlife Refuge

For nature lovers, a visit to the Nosara Wildlife Refuge is a must. This protected area is home to a variety of wildlife, including monkeys, birds, and iguanas. Take a guided tour to learn about the local ecosystem and spot wildlife in their natural habitats.

###### 4. Ziplining through the Jungle

Experience the thrill of ziplining through the lush Costa Rican jungle. Several tour operators in Nosara offer ziplining excursions that give you a bird's-eye view of the jungle canopy, rivers, and wildlife.

###### 5. Horseback Riding on the Beach

For a unique perspective of Nosara, try horseback riding along the beach. This peaceful activity allows you to take in the beauty of the coastline while enjoying the company of your horse.

###### 6. Rent an ATV or Golf Cart to Explore

One of the best ways to explore the area around Nosara is by renting an ATV or golf cart. These vehicles make it easy to navigate the rugged terrain and discover hidden gems, from secluded beaches to local cafes and shops. Many rental shops in Nosara offer both ATVs and golf carts, so you can choose the perfect ride for your adventure.
